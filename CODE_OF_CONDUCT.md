# Code Of Conduct

Smartie contributors are expected to keep project discussions technical,
respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Critique code, behavior, and design tradeoffs rather than people.
- Keep bug reports reproducible and privacy-aware.
- Do not pressure users to share private recordings, telemetry, credentials, or
  sensitive screenshots publicly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private data.
- Repeated low-signal comments after maintainers ask for a thread to stay on
  topic.
- Using project spaces to bypass the noncommercial license restriction.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block participants who
make the project unsafe or unproductive. Security and privacy reports should
follow `SECURITY.md`.
